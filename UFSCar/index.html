              • WO2024020657: COMPOSS DE COMPLEXES MTALLIQUES, PROCD DE SYNTHSE DES COMPOSS, FILMS POLYMRE COMPRENANT LES COMPOSS ET LEURS UTILISATIONS

                Abstract: (2024)

                The present invention comprises the development of compounds obtained from the formation of magnesium metal complexes with antioxidant activity, the process for synthesising the compounds thus obtained, the polymeric films comprising said compounds and the uses thereof. More specifically, said compounds have the general formula [M(LL)(L'L')n]+, wherein: M is the magnesium metal (Mg(II)); LL is an antioxidant agent; L'L' is a chelating agent, wherein n = 1 or 2. Said compounds, regardless of the choice of oxidants and chelating agents, are prepared preferably via equimolar reaction of an appropriate salt of Mg(II), and the ligands LL and L'L' in the presence or absence of triethylamine. The present invention further claims the use of said compounds in the formation of natural polymeric films for conserving foods and drinks rich in riboflavin, the use of said compounds in the preparation of an insecticide, preferably against leafcutter ants (Atta sexdens), and also their use in the preparation of fungicides and bactericides, preferentially against the symbiotic fungus (Leucoagaricus gongylophorus) of leafcutter ants. Furthermore, the use of the compound in the preparation of a cosmetic composition, preferably aimed at anti-aging, is also demonstrated. Finally, the use of said compound as a stabiliser in insulin formulations is also proven with promising data.
